Cognitive Restructuring

It's a therapeutic process used to identify and confront negative thought patterns and help people understand that these thoughts are ineffective or disruptive, with the goal to ultimately change negative behaviors. It teaches patients how to think differently by replacing adverse and illogical thoughts ("faulty thinking") with more rational and positive types of thinking.

BENEFITS...

  • Lowers Symptoms of Depression.
  • Reduces Anxiety.
  • Helps Treat Eating Disorders.
  • Helps Improve Self-Esteem and Confidence.
  • Makes we realise how important we are to this beautiful life.
